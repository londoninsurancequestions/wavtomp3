Good to know

Questions, answered.

Will this work with scanned PDFs?+
Calibre converts text it can extract from the PDF. Pure image scans (photos of pages) need OCR first — try Adobe Acrobat, ocrmypdf, or similar, then convert the searchable PDF here.
Can I send the EPUB straight to my Kindle?+
Download the EPUB, then use Amazon’s “Send to Kindle” app or email it to your Kindle address. For easiest Kindle transfer, some users prefer converting to AZW3 locally in Calibre — this tool outputs standard EPUB.
What about DRM-protected PDFs?+
We can’t convert copy-protected files. Only use documents you own or have permission to reformat.
How is this different from reflow in Apple Books or Kindle?+
Built-in PDF viewers still treat the file as a fixed page. EPUB stores real chapters and HTML text, so line breaks adapt to your font size and screen — a much better long-form reading experience.
Is my file kept on your servers?+
No. The PDF is processed in a temporary workspace and deleted as soon as conversion completes. We don’t build a library from your uploads on this page.
