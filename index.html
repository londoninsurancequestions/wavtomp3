Two ways to convert

Your files, your call.

Every converter runs the same way — local encoding in your browser by default, with server mode one click away for big jobs.

Local mode

Audio is decoded and re-encoded on your machine. Nothing is sent over the network — perfect for sensitive recordings and client work.

Server mode

Off-load massive batches to dedicated workers. Uploads are deleted automatically once you've downloaded the results.

Full control

Bitrate, sample rate, channels, loudness normalization, trimming, and metadata — exposed as controls you can actually understand.
